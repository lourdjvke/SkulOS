# SchoolOS

![SchoolOS Logo](https://getillustrations.b-cdn.net/photos/pack/Sketch-illustrations-travel-character_lg.png)

Welcome to **SchoolOS**, the all-in-one operating system designed to streamline school data management and empower educators.

## What is SchoolOS?

SchoolOS is a centralized platform that brings together student data, staff management, and administrative workflows into a single, intuitive interface. Say goodbye to fragmented spreadsheets and hello to a unified workspace.

---

## Key Features

### 🧠 AI-Powered Copilot
Our intelligent assistant helps you query data, build structures, and manage files effortlessly. Just ask, and SchoolOS delivers.

![Copilot Illustration](https://framerusercontent.com/images/xUBbN7T4rif5aABumrXdvQiqWA.png?scale-down-to=1024&width=1040&height=1040)

### 📂 Intelligent Workspace
Organize your school's documents, tables, and resources with a flexible folder structure. Keep everything where it belongs.

### 👥 Staff Management
Easily manage staff information, track data logs, and monitor weekly progress in one place.

### 🚀 Seamless Collaboration
Share files, tag resources, and work together with your team without the friction of traditional systems.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Sign In**: Access your school's workspace using your credentials.
2. **Setup**: Configure your school profile and invite staff members.
3. **Explore**: Use the AI Copilot to start managing your data immediately.
